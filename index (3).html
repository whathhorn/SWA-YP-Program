How to find your calling

Adam Leipzig  ·  TED Talk  ·  5 min

Leipzig asks five simple questions that cut through everything: Who are you? What do you do? Who do you do it for? What do they want or need? What do they get out of it? In five minutes he gives most people more clarity on their purpose than they've had in years. The shortest thing on this entire list — and arguably the most immediately useful.
